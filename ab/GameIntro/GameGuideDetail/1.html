1. Starting the game  

In this chapter, we’ll look into:
1. How to create an account
2. How to create a character
3. How to set your nickname

▣ Login account
If you are new to the game, you can choose to log into Facebook, Google, or Apple to create an account and play the game. Otherwise, you can continue as a guest account.

 

▣ Facebook Account
If you chose a Facebook account, you could log in directly with your Facebook account.
If you don’t have a Facebook account, you can use it after creating a new account.

Create a Facebook account from the link below;
https://www.facebook.com/

 

▣ Google Account
If you chose a Google account, you can log in directly with your Google account.
If you don’t have a Google account, you can use it after creating a new account.

Create a Google account from the link below; 
https://accounts.google.com/signup/v2/webcreateaccount?hl=en&flowName=GlifWebSignIn&flowEntry=SignUp 


 

▣ Apple Account
If you chose an Apple account, you can log in directly with your Apple account.
If you don’t have an Apple account, you can use it after creating a new account.

You can create an Apple account through iPhone, iPad, iTunes, etc. 

 

▣ Guest Account
In the case of a guest account, you can use it immediately without a separate platform sign-up process. However, when using a guest account, you may lose all gameplay data if you delete the game, change your device, experience errors, etc. 
We highly recommend that you link your account with Google, Apple, or Facebook.


 

▣ Lobby Screen
This is the screen you can see after logging in.
You can change servers or start the game by clicking the game Start.

Tap “Account” at the top left to log out or change your account.
Tap “News” to check the latest announcements from Ragnarok Origin. 
Tap “Intro” to see the intro video of Ragnarok Origin.
Tap “Customer Service” to send inquiries to the customer service team.


 


▣ Character Creation
When you first access the game, you’ll be directed to the character creation screen.
You can choose the gender of your character by clicking the relevant button at the bottom of the screen. 


 

After choosing the gender, you can choose the appearance of your character.
Tap the buttons on the right and left to change the shape and color of your hair and eyes.
Whenever you’re ready, tap the Start Adventure button to start the game.



 

▣ Nickname Setting
The first thing you need to do in the world of Ragnarok Origin is to register yourself as a Novice.
Talk to NPC Sprakki to begin the registration process. 


 

Input your character name and tap the Confirm Registration button to complete the process.


 

Congratulations on your official registration at Novice Academy!
